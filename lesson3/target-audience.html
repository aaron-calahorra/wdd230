Target Audience

This website will be mostly be used by business owners in the city that want to support or get help from other entrepreneurs. This audience could be described in two personas:

  • Business owners that are looking for opportunities to create strong relationships with entrepreneurs from their city in order to improve their products, services and prices. They will be looking for news and scan interesting keywords. They will visit the website from their phones.
  • Neighbors discovering businesses near to them. They are looking for innovative and fresh ideas to try and share with their friends and family. They will find a community of entrepreneurs who support each other and their business, and that will increase their trust in local businesses.

Scenarios

  1. How can I contact new partners and suppliers for my business?
  2. What's the telephone number of the new restaurant across the street?
  3. Does other business owners have the same problems that I'm having right now?
  4. Where could I find if my business idea will have a lot of competition?
  5. Where to find a place where I could pay less rent?
